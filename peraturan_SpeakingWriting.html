TOEIC Test Rules

Please carefully review all guidelines before beginning your examination

General Test Rules

1
Time Limits: The Reading section has a 75-minute time limit, and the Listening section has a 45-minute time limit. Once you start a section, the timer cannot be paused.
2
No External Materials: You may not use dictionaries, textbooks, notes, or any other external resources during the test.
3
No Assistance: You must complete the test independently without help from anyone else.
4
Answer All Questions: Try to answer all questions. There is no penalty for incorrect answers.
5
No Application Switching: Do not switch to other applications during the test. Remain in the test application until you complete each section.

Reading Section

1
Passage Navigation: You can navigate between different parts of the reading materials as needed while answering questions.
2
Question Types: You will encounter various question types including incomplete sentences, text completion, and reading comprehension.
3
Question Navigation: You can move freely between questions in this section. Use the question number buttons to navigate.

Listening Section

1
Audio Playback Limit: Each audio recording can only be played ONCE. Ensure your audio is working properly before starting.
2
No Rewinding: You cannot pause, rewind, or replay any part of the audio once it starts playing.
3
Take Notes: You are encouraged to take notes during the audio playback to help answer questions.
4
Question Order: Questions must be answered in the order they appear. You cannot return to previous questions once you move forward.

Important Note

If you close the application during the test, your progress may be lost. Ensure your computer has sufficient battery power and you have allocated enough time to complete each section before starting.
